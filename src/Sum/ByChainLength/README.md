# Sum by chain length

- [Sum of short chain fatty acids](LongChain.md)
    - SCFA
    - are fatty acids with aliphatic tails of 5 or fewer carbons
- [Sum of medium chain fatty acids](MediumChain.md)
    - MCFA
    - are fatty acids with aliphatic tails of 6 to 12 carbons
- [Sum of long chain fatty acids](ShortChain.md)
    - LCFA
    - are fatty acids with aliphatic tails of 13 to 21 carbons
- [Sum of very long chain fatty acids](VeryLongChain.md)
    - VLCFA
    - are fatty acids with aliphatic tails of 22 or more carbons
