# Sum of antiatherogenic fatty acids

`nutritional`

$$\sum \ce{MUFA} + \sum \ce{PUFA(\omega-6)} + \sum \ce{PUFA(\omega-3)}$$

## Description

Represents the sum of unsaturated fatty acids that exhibit antiatherogenic properties. These fatty acids help inhibit the aggregation of plaque and reduce the levels of phospholipids and cholesterol in the cardiovascular system, thereby lowering the risk of coronary heart disease.
