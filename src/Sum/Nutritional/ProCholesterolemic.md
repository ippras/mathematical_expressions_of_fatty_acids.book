# Sum of procholesterolemic fatty acids

`nutritional`

$$\ce{C{12:0}} + \ce{C{14:0}} + \ce{C{16:0}}$$

## Synonyms

- Sum of hypercholesterolaemic fatty acids
- Sum of hypercholesterolemic fatty acids

## Description

Represents the sum of saturated fatty acids that have a strong hypercholesterolemic effect. Consumption of these fatty acids (lauric, myristic, and palmitic acids) is associated with elevated levels of total cholesterol and low-density lipoprotein (LDL) cholesterol in the blood plasma, which can negatively impact cardiovascular health.
