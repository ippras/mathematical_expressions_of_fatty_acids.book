# Sum of antithrombogenic fatty acids

`nutritional`

$$\sum \ce{MUFA} + \sum \ce{PUFA(\omega-6)} + \sum \ce{PUFA(\omega-3)}$$

## Description

Represents the sum of unsaturated fatty acids that possess antithrombogenic properties. These fatty acids help prevent the formation of blood clots (thrombogenesis) in blood vessels, thereby reducing the risk of cardiovascular events such as stroke and myocardial infarction.
