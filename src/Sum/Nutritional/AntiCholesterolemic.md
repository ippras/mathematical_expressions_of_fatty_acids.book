# Sum of anticholesterolemic fatty acids

`nutritional`

$$\ce{C{18:1}(\omega-9)} + \sum \ce{PUFA}$$

## Synonyms

- Sum of hypocholesterolaemic fatty acids
- Sum of hypocholesterolemic fatty acids

## Description

Represents the sum of fatty acids that actively contribute to lowering blood cholesterol levels. This group primarily includes oleic acid and polyunsaturated fatty acids, which are associated with a reduction in low-density lipoprotein (LDL) cholesterol and an overall improvement in the lipid profile, protecting against cardiovascular diseases.
