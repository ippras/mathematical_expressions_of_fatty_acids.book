# Sum of prothrombogenic fatty acids

`nutritional`

$$\ce{C{14:0}} + \ce{C{16:0}} + \ce{C{18:0}}$$

## Description

Represents the sum of specific saturated fatty acids (myristic, palmitic, and stearic acids) that exhibit prothrombogenic potential. These fatty acids promote the activation of platelets and the formation of blood clots within the cardiovascular system, increasing the risk of thrombosis and related cardiovascular complications.
