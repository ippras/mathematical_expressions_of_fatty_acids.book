# Sum of eicosapentaenoic and docosahexaenoic fatty acids

$$\ce{C{20:5}(\omega-3)} + \ce{C{22:6}(\omega-3)}$$

## Abbreviations

- EPA + DHA

## Description

Represents the sum of eicosapentaenoic acid (EPA) and docosahexaenoic acid (DHA) content. These are the two most critical marine-derived essential omega-3 long-chain polyunsaturated fatty acids. This sum is frequently used as a key indicator of the nutritional quality of seafood, microalgae, and enriched foods, reflecting their potential to provide significant cardiovascular, anti-inflammatory, and cognitive health benefits.
