# General health index of cheese

## Abbreviations

- GHIC

## Description

Represents the General Health Index of Cheese (GHIC), an indicator designed to evaluate the presence of health-promoting compounds in cheese. It compacts the contributions of various beneficial components—such as specific polyunsaturated fatty acids (including CLA and omega-3 PUFAs), polyphenols, and total antioxidant capacity—into a single value. This index is particularly useful for assessing the nutritional and health-promoting quality of dairy products obtained from animals fed with fresh forage or pasture.
