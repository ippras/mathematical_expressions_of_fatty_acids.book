# Long chain saturated factor

$$LCSF = 0.1 \times C16:0 + 0.5 \times C18:0 + 1 \times C20:0 + 1.5 \times C22:0 + 2 \times C24:0$$

## Abbreviations

- LCSF

## Description

Represents the long chain saturated factor, which is used to evaluate the cold flow properties of biodiesel. It assigns increasing weight to saturated fatty acids with longer carbon chains, as they have progressively higher melting points and more severely impact the cold filter plugging point (CFPP). A higher LCSF indicates poorer cold-weather operability.
