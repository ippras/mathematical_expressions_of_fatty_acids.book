# Cold filter plugging point

$$CFPP = 3.1417 \times LCSF - 16.477$$

## Abbreviations

- CFPP

## Description

Represents the cold filter plugging point (CFPP), which is one important parameter for low temperature applications of a fuel. The CFPP is the lowest temperature at which a fuel will still flow through a specific filter before jamming it due to the formation of agglomerated crystals. It is heavily influenced by the long chain saturated factor (LCSF).

**Biodiesel standards**:

The EN 14214-2005 standard does not mention a low-temperature parameter in its specification lists. However, each country using EN 14214 can specify certain temperature limits for different times of a year depending on climate conditions.
