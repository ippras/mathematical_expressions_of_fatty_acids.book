# Degree of unsaturation

$$DU = 1-UFA + 2 \times 2-UFA + 3 \times 3-UFA + 4 \times 4-UFA$$

## Abbreviations

- DU

## Description

Represents the degree of unsaturation (DU), which quantifies the total amount of double bonds present in the fatty acid profile of raw oils or biodiesel. Fuel properties such as cetane number (CN), iodine value (IV), and oxidation stability (OS) are mainly determined by the DU. When the degree of unsaturation of raw oils fatty acid is lower than 133.13, cetane number (GB/T 20828-2007) and iodine number (EN 14214) generally meet the required standards.
