# Linoleic fatty acid to α-Linolenic fatty acid ratio

`nutritional`

$$\frac{\ce{C{18:2}(\omega-6)}}{\ce{C{18:3}(\omega-3)}}$$

## Abbreviations

- LA/ALA

## Description

Represents the ratio of linoleic acid (an omega-6 fatty acid) to α-linolenic acid (an omega-3 fatty acid). This index is crucial for evaluating the balance of essential fatty acids in the diet and is widely used for guiding infant formula development to ensure optimal neurological and visual development. Maintaining an appropriate LA/ALA ratio is important for regulating inflammatory responses and overall metabolic health.

**Limits**:

- below 4:1 is not recommended [[1]](#references)
- from 5:1 to 15:1 [[5]](#references)
