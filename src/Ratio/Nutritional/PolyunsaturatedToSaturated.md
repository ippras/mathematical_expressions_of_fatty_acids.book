# Polyunsaturated fatty acids to saturated fatty acids ratio

$$\frac{\sum \ce{PUFA}}{\sum \ce{SFA}}$$

## Abbreviations

- PUFA/SFA

## Description

Represents the ratio of polyunsaturated fatty acids to saturated fatty acids. It is a widely used nutritional index to assess the impact of diet on cardiovascular health (CVH). A higher PUFA/SFA ratio is generally considered beneficial, as it indicates a greater proportion of heart-healthy polyunsaturated fats relative to saturated fats, which are often associated with increased cardiovascular risk.
